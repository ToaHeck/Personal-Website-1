About me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I am To'a Heck, a senior at California State University - East Bay, set to graduate in the fall of 2023. As a person of Polynesian descent with Samoan and Tongan heritage, I am proud to represent my culture in the tech industry. Unfortunately, there is a lack of representation of the Polynesian population in tech, which makes my role even more important to me.

Currently, I work at Topcon Positioning Systems in Livermore, CA as an IT Applications intern. My responsibilites include creating IT solutions for customers as well as managing and maintaining user databases. One of the projects I am currently working on is reviewing legacy code for an application written in Classic ASP. I've spent the last couple of months blueprinting the web app and in the coming weeks, I will begin to migrate it to the ASP.Net suite. Through this experience, I have gained expertise in server-side scripting and how ADO objects are created and maintained to update a database.

During my free time, I love to indulge in my creative pursuits, which include playing the guitar, working out at the gym, and designing artwork using tools like Photoshop, After Effects, Blender, and Premiere Pro. As an artist, I have had the opportunity to create compelling artwork for musical artists, such as album art, music videos, and concert posters. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and my work!
