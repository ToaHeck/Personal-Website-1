About me

My name is To'a Heck and I am currently a senior at California State University - East Bay. I will be graduating in the fall of 2023 and am very excited. I am half Samoan and half Tongan, making me of Polynesian descent. I am proud of my culture and am privilaged to represent it in the tech industry as I have noticed that there is very little representation of the Polynesian population in tech.

I currently work at Topcon Positioning Systems in Livermore, CA as an IT Applications intern. My job consists of reviewing old code written for web aplications in Classic ASP as well as completing and triaging Jira tickes. I have learned server-side scripting as well as how ADO objects are created and maintained to update a database.

In my free time, I enjoy playing guitar, training in the gym, and creating artwork within Photoshop, After Effects, Blender, and Premiere Pro. In the past, I have been commisioned to create artwork for musical artist. This consists of album art, music videos, and concert posters.
